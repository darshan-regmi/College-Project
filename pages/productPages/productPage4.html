Rolex Datejust 41

Iconic luxury timepiece with impeccable precision

NPR 6,500,000.00

Product Description

The Rolex Datejust 41 is a modern classic, renowned for its elegant design and flawless performance. With a 41mm case, the watch features a striking silver dial, a date function, and a Cyclops lens for easy date reading. Powered by the self-winding movement, this timepiece ensures unparalleled accuracy and reliability. The signature Oystersteel case and jubilee bracelet make this model as durable as it is beautiful.

Key Features:

  • 41mm stainless steel case for a robust feel
  • Flawless silver dial with date function
  • Oystersteel case for enhanced durability
  • Self-winding automatic movement for accuracy
  • Iconic Rolex Cyclops lens for easy date visibility

Specifications:

Dial Shape Round
Case Material Oystersteel
Case Diameter 41mm
Strap Material Jubilee Bracelet
Water Resistance 100 meters
