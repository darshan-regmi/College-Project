Built to withstand tough conditions, perfect for extreme adventures

Product Description

The Casio G-Shock Mudmaster is designed for those who thrive in harsh environments. Whether you're trekking through rugged terrain or facing the elements, this watch provides durability and accuracy. With features such as shock resistance, water resistance up to 200 meters, and a mud-resistant design, it's built to handle whatever comes your way. The bold design, featuring a digital-analog display, ensures you have everything you need to keep moving forward.

Key Features:

  • Shock resistant design for tough conditions
  • Mud-resistant for extreme outdoor use
  • Water-resistant up to 200 meters
  • Digital and analog display for versatility
  • Built-in compass, altimeter, and barometer for outdoor activities

Specifications:

Case Material Resin
Display Type Digital & Analog
Water Resistance 200 meters
Battery Life Up to 2 years
