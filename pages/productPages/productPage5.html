Breguet Type XX

Classic aviation-inspired chronograph

Product Description

The Breguet Type XX is a timeless chronograph inspired by aviation. With its elegant and functional design, this watch offers precise timekeeping and a high-quality finish. The black dial with luminous hands, coupled with the tachymeter bezel, makes it perfect for both casual and professional settings. The robust stainless steel case and leather strap provide lasting durability and comfort.

Key Features:

  • Black dial with luminous hands for easy readability
  • Stainless steel case for durability
  • Leather strap for comfort and style
  • Chronograph function with tachymeter bezel
  • Classic aviation-inspired design

Specifications:

Dial Shape Round
Case Material Stainless Steel
Case Diameter 39mm
Strap Material Leather
Water Resistance 3 ATM
